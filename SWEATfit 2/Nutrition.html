Nutrition

When it comes to body goals and fitness, 80% of your results will be achieved in the kitchen. People seem to downplay it but here at SWEAT we aim to focus on the importance of nutrition to give you the most attainable way to achieve your goals AND sustain them.

Macro Counting

We think counting your calories and macros is the best way to achieve your body goals as this mathematically calculates your body maintenance calories(how much your body needs to stay the same weight/size) then by simply adding or subtracting 500 calories you can see yourself either gain weight or lose weight(bulking/cutting) to achieve your desired look. It then goes further as we then split those calories into carbs, proteins and fats, for example if your were trying to bulk muscle, you'd add 500 calories toward your maintenance caloric intake and have a higher protein split. While on the other end of the spectrum if you were trying to lose wight(cut) you would subtract 500 calories from your maintenance calories and have a lower carb split. We have a calculator tool below to help you find out your caloric needs and the split you may prefer with a cook book on the side which will give you ideas on what foods to make to help your journey.
